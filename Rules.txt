This is a game created in Unity. It features a First person Player. The Basic concept of the game is to retrieve certain elements from the chests, firstly a lantern, a gun to shoot enemies, and a key to open the door and get out of the game. To pickup elements please press "E" to open the chest when you see a hand icon and press "E"  again to pick it up. to switch between using the gun and key, press "G" when you want to use the gun and press "K" when you want to use the key. When the enemies are close press spacebar to shoot using gun. If incase the enemies touch you, you are dead and have to retry again.
